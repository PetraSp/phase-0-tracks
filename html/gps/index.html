About bread

Bread is a staple food prepared from a dough of flour and water, usually by baking. Throughout recorded history it has been popular around the world and is one of the oldest artificial foods, having been of importance since the dawn of agriculture.

Not all bread is created equal. You don’t need to be a baker, a foodie or even fully awake to notice that tearing into artisan, freshly-baked loaf is a lot nicer than the supermarket sliced variety – but beyond that, things can get confusing.

Artisan bread

Artisan Breads are hand-crafted, hearth-baked loaves of yeast bread.

Is bread bad for you?

Different breads have different properties. Instead of avoiding bread altogether, knowing what is in each slice and watching how much of it you eat each day is usually the healthier course.
